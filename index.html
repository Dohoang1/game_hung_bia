Pepe is sad. Help him become happy again by catching as much beer as possible!

How to Play

  • Use the left and right arrow keys to move the basket.
  • Catch the falling beers to score points.
  • If you miss a beer, you lose a life.
  • Every 10 points, level up, increase the difficulty of the game and gain an extra life.
  • Game over when you run out of lives.
  • Have fun!
